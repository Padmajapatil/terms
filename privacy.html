PRIVACY POLICY

When you use visa (App), some personal information is collected from and about you. We are committed to protecting the security of this information and safeguarding your privacy. At registration you accept the terms of this Privacy Policy and your use of the App signifies your continued acceptance thereof. This Privacy Policy may be revised from time to time and you will be notified of all such changes. In order to use the App, you will be required to consent to the terms of the Privacy Policy as revised from time to time.

This Policy applies to all registered users/Visitors of app including individuals whose personal information may be collected, stored and used for Visa purposes by app. This privacy policy sets out the details of the personal information collected, the manner in which it is collected, by whom as well as the purposes for which it is used.

INFORMATION COLLECTED AND MANNER OF COLLECTION



The types of personal information collected will depend on the nature of the services and may include your name, contact details, photographic information, demographic details, family details, travel information, etc.

When you install and register on the UKI App, the following set of information is collected from you and stored securely on a server: personal details, passport details, address, family details, profession details, visa sought, other countries visited, references in India, photo, passport copy, and relevant documents, etc. This information will be stored on the server, and a unique digital ID for your identification will be pushed to your app. This digital ID will then be used to identify you in all subsequent app-related transactions and will be associated with any data or information uploaded from the app to the server.

USE OF INFORMATION


If you register to seek any Indian visa-related service, some personal information will need to be collected for this purpose.

The personal information collected at the time of registration, while filling out the visa application, or seeking any other service through the UKI App will be stored on a server and only used for processing your application and rendering the required service.

No attempt will be made to identify users or their browsing activities except in the unlikely event of an investigation where a law enforcement agency may exercise a warrant to inspect server logs. The statistics and log files may be preserved indefinitely and used at any time and in any way necessary to prevent security breaches and to ensure the integrity of the information supplied.

The information collected will only be used by Indian Immigration and Visa authorities in a secure manner.

RETENTION


The information collected will be stored on servers for a certain period as decided by the relevant authorities.

This means that any personal information provided through the UKI App, whether during registration, while filling out visa applications, or seeking other services, will be securely stored on these servers. The duration for which your information will be kept is determined by the policies and regulations set by the authorities. After this period, appropriate measures will be taken to ensure the secure disposal or archiving of the data, in compliance with applicable data protection and privacy laws.

This approach ensures that your information is handled responsibly and used solely for the purposes for which it was collected.

RIGHTS

As a registered user, you have the right to access your profile at any time to add, remove, or modify any registration information that you have supplied.

However, you cannot manage the communications that you receive from us or how you receive them. If you no longer wish to receive communications from us, you may cancel your registration through the UKI App. This means that while you have control over the personal information in your profile, the preferences for receiving updates or notifications from the app are not customizable. Should you decide that you no longer want to be contacted, you have the option to terminate your registration, thereby ceasing all communications from our side.

DATA SECURITY


The UKI App is equipped with standard security features to protect the confidentiality and security of your information.

Sensitive profile data is saved in an encrypted format, ensuring that no unauthorized person can view this information. The app employs robust encryption methods to safeguard your personal details, making sure that your data remains secure from potential breaches or unauthorized access. Additionally, multiple layers of security protocols are implemented to maintain the integrity and privacy of the information you provide. This ensures that your data is handled with the highest level of security, giving you peace of mind when using the app.

DISCLOSURES AND TRANSFER


No personal information collected by the UKI App will be disclosed or transferred to any third party.

This means that any data you provide during the registration process, while filling out applications, or when using other services through the app, will be kept strictly confidential. The app is designed to ensure that your personal information remains private and is not shared with external entities under any circumstances. Your data will only be used for the purposes for which it was collected and will be securely stored, protecting it from unauthorized access or disclosure. This commitment to privacy ensures that your information is handled responsibly and with the utmost care.
